Our Objective

ORION is designed to analyze Control Narrative documents and convert their content into structured engineering insight. After a Control Narrative PDF is uploaded, the system identifies the different equipment described in the document, understands their associated logic and conditions, and generates clear, structured pseudocode that represents the intended control behavior for each identified element.
